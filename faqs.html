FAQS

1: What are the operating hours of the fitness center?

The fitness center is open 24 hours a day, 7 days a week.

2: Can I access the fitness center at any time?

Yes, our fitness center is accessible to members at all times, allowing for flexible workout schedules.

3: Do you offer group exercise classes?

Yes, we offer a variety of group exercise classes throughout the week. Please check our schedule for class timings and availability.

4: Are personal trainers available at the fitness center?

Yes, we have certified personal trainers who can provide guidance and personalized training sessions. Additional fees may apply.

5: What amenities are available at the fitness center?

Our fitness center offers a range of amenities, including cardio and strength training equipment, locker rooms with showers, and towel service.

6: Is there parking available?

Yes, we have dedicated parking spaces available for members. Please make sure to follow any posted parking guidelines.

7: Can I bring a guest to the fitness center?

Yes, we offer guest passes for members to bring a friend or family member. Guest policies and fees may apply.

8: Are there any age restrictions for using the fitness center?

Members must be at least 18 years old to access the fitness center. However, we may have special programs or areas designated for younger members.

9: Can I freeze or cancel my membership?

Yes, we have options to freeze or cancel your membership. Please contact our customer service or visit the front desk for assistance.

10: How can I sign up for a membership?

You can sign up for a membership online through our website or visit our front desk during staffed hours for assistance.
